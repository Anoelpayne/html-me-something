The Pedaler's Jamboree

The Pedaler's Jamboree (aka ped jam) is a 30 mile bicycle ride on the Katy Trail from Columbia to Boonville that takes place Memorial weekend. There are 3 stops along the way with food trucks, beer tents, and live music. Thousands of people from all over Missouri gather to ride. Bringing all different personalities together to make for a truly memorable weekend. We ride rain or shine (and the occational tornado alarm). 2021 will be the 5th year I, along with some of my favorite people, will have participated. It is my ABSOLUTE FAVORITE TIME OF YEAR! And I am excited to show you why!

The yearly weather report

    2015: Rain, rain, and more rain
    2016: Storms in the morning, then mostly cloudy
    2017: Tornado warnings!! (had to take shelter under a tree!)
    2018: Sun! Sun! Sun... wait... 97 degrees (be careful what you wish for)
    2019: sunny, 75 degrees, light breeze, and that makes for a perfect day!!
    2020: Canceled due to a global pandemic.

The first stop

The first of three rest stops is located at Katfish Katy's. By this point we have gone 10 miles and energies are still high and lively. Katfish Katy's offer great food and live music!

The second stop

The second stop is located in Roachport. We have now gone 15 miles and legs are starting to feel a little wobbly. This halfway stop is right on the Missouri river and has various food trucks with yet another live band playing.

The third stop

The third stop is in New Franklin and we are almost done! With only 4 miles left to go we start to really get excited and maybe have a few drinks. More live music and a dance performance!

We made it!!

30 miles later and we are sore, tired, and pretty much giddy with pride and excitement. When we arrive in Booneville our camping gear is there waiting for us. After we set up our tents, it's time to head down to the main field and refresh our drinks and order dinner from a food truck. There is a main stage with EVEN MORE LIVE MUSIC and the best fireworks show I've ever seen!!
